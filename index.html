Title Tags: ID.4 + Tiguan 2026

Reduce 5 H1 tags to 1. Differentiate duplicate content from /ourteam.html (85%+ identical). Add explicit canonical. Recovers brand query rankings and prevents de-indexing of the primary URL.

30-minute implementation that directs ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews to the right inventory pages. Site currently returns 404. Zero direct local competitors have this in place.
